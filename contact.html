FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project engagement model?

We typically work on fixed-scope, fixed-price engagements for product development, with optional ongoing support contracts. For compliance audits and privacy engineering, we work on a time-and-materials basis with monthly minimums.

Where are your engineers based?

Our core team is based at St John's Innovation Centre in Cambridge, UK. We have additional engineering presence across Europe and work with a small number of trusted specialist contractors globally.

Do you take equity or revenue-share partnerships?

We are open to discussing partnership arrangements for projects where our technology stack provides exceptional strategic value. Please reach out via contact@echodatagridapex.com with a brief project overview.

How quickly can you start a new project?

New project intake typically begins 2-4 weeks from contract signing, depending on scope. For urgent compliance or audit work, we maintain limited availability for fast-track engagements.

Can I report a security vulnerability in your apps?

Absolutely — we operate a coordinated vulnerability disclosure program. Please email contact@echodatagridapex.com with details. We acknowledge all reports within 48 hours and aim to triage within 5 business days.

Do you provide user support in languages other than English?

Our primary support language is English. For our localized apps in Japanese, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Chinese, and Korean markets, we provide regional support through our trusted partner network.
