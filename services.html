Our Capabilities

Services Built on Three Pillars

We deliver end-to-end digital product engineering — from procedural technology R&D and immersive interaction design to monetization infrastructure and global compliance. Every service is anchored in our privacy-first philosophy.

Service 01

Programmatic Technology R&D

Our core competency. We research, prototype, and ship production-grade procedural generation systems for audio, visual, and interaction layers.

From real-time generative audio engines to infinite-variety visual layouts, we build the algorithms that make our products feel alive, fresh, and personal.

  • Procedural audio synthesis with parametric seed generation
  • Algorithmic visual composition for UI and asset generation
  • Real-time interaction graphs for organic user experiences
  • Custom DSL development for game/app logic composition
  • Spaced-repetition learning algorithms with adaptive scheduling
  • Computer vision for offline document & sheet music scanning
  • On-device machine learning with federated training principles
  • Algorithmic music theory engines for composition & analysis
  • Smart categorization systems for expense and inventory data
Service 02

Privacy-First App Development

We design, build, and publish complete iOS and Android applications — from initial concept through App Store submission and ongoing lifecycle management.

Every app in our portfolio shares the same architectural foundation: local-first data, encrypted at rest, no required cloud sync, no third-party tracking.

  • Native iOS development in Swift / SwiftUI
  • Native Android development in Kotlin / Jetpack Compose
  • Cross-platform React Native & Flutter when appropriate
  • App Store & Google Play submission, metadata & ASO
  • Privacy-respecting analytics (zero PII, on-device aggregation)
  • Crash reporting with full PII redaction
  • Beta distribution via TestFlight & Google Play internal testing
  • Continuous integration with automated compliance checks
  • Lifecycle management, version planning, and feature rollouts
  • Localization for 20+ languages with regional compliance
Service 03

IAA & IAP Monetization Engineering

We architect and integrate full monetization stacks across in-app advertising (IAA) and in-app purchases (IAP) — designed for compliance, user experience, and revenue optimization.

From ad mediation waterfalls to subscription management, we handle the entire monetization infrastructure with rigorous attention to user experience.

  • Google AdMob integration with full SDK compliance
  • AppLovin MAX mediation waterfall configuration
  • Unity LevelPlay (ironSource) hybrid monetization
  • Meta Audience Network for diversified demand
  • Pangle (TikTok) for global Tier-2/Tier-3 reach
  • Mintegral, InMobi, TapJoy, Chartboost, Vungle integrations
  • Splash, banner, interstitial, rewarded video, native formats
  • Apple StoreKit 2 subscription management with 24h renewal reminders
  • Google Play Billing v6 with real-time developer notifications
  • AppsFlyer / Adjust / Singular attribution & fraud prevention
  • SKAdNetwork & Privacy Sandbox post-IDFA architecture
  • Server-side receipt validation with retry logic
Service 04

2026 Global Compliance Engineering

We design, implement, and audit full-stack compliance solutions covering GDPR, UK-GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, VCDPA, LGPD, PIPEDA, DPDP, and the EU Digital Services Act.

Our compliance engineering covers technical implementation, legal documentation, data residency architecture, and ongoing regulatory monitoring.

  • GDPR / UK-GDPR data subject rights implementation
  • CCPA / CPRA "Do Not Sell" mechanisms and opt-out flows
  • VCDPA, CPA, CTDPA state-by-state compliance
  • EU DSA transparency reports and content moderation tooling
  • EU AI Act high-risk system classification & documentation
  • Brazil LGPD consent management and ANPD reporting
  • India DPDP Act consent collection and data principal rights
  • China PIPL data localization and CAC security assessments
  • Saudi Arabia PDPL NDPA registration and local hosting
  • Canada PIPEDA / Quebec Law 25 privacy impact assessments
  • Japan APPI cross-border transfer documentation
  • Children's data (COPPA, UK Age-Appropriate Design Code)
Service 05

Immersive Interaction Design

We craft interfaces that feel less like tools and more like companions. Our design philosophy blends Zen-inspired minimalism with rigorous functional discipline.

Every animation, transition, and interaction is purposeful — designed to create moments of quiet focus rather than demand attention.

  • Motion design systems with spring-physics animations
  • Haptic feedback engineering for tactile interaction
  • Adaptive theming respecting system dark/light modes
  • Accessibility (WCAG 2.2 AA) & inclusive design audits
  • Sound design for UI feedback & ambient experience
  • Micro-interaction prototyping with Principle & Origami
  • Information architecture for content-heavy applications
  • Usability testing & qualitative research programs
  • Brand system development with respect for whitespace
